Dean Tunbridge Blog

Hello my name is Dean Tunbridge, I am originally from the UK but I have now lived in Auckland for about 7 years. I am attending Dev Academy as I am ready for a career change after a decade in the kitchen. I have done some self study courses over the past couple of years and absolutely loved it, so I felt ready to commit to full time study.

Learning to become a dev is probably going to be the hardest thing I've ever tried to do in my life, which excites me and scares me in equal measures. This blog contains everything I have learned in the foundations course.
