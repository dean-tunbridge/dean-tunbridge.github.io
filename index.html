About Me

Originally from Devon, England, I've lived in NZ for almost 10 years. My previous career gave the chance to live all over the world including France, Australia, Canada, Sweden, and Thailand. I wrote my first line of code in 2018 and it became an instant hobby. I decided to turn this passion into a career change, so as soon as I got my residency, I studied software development at Dev Academy and graduated in late 2024. Since then I've worked on personal coding projects non-stop, as well as doing side projects with friends who are in the industry.
