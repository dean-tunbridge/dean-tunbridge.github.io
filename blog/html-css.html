What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

The main difference between padding and margin is that padding controls how much breathing room exists within a box, while margin controls how much breathing room or whitespace exists outside of a box. The gap between this paragraph and the box below is created by the margin for example.

I have built an example box here with border, padding and margin. The border is represented with red colouring and is 20px, whilst the padding is the space inside the box, and it is represented by the light blue colouring and is 80px. Finally, the margin is the space outside and around the box and is 100px.
